## Status Values (FINALIZED)
- **New** - Estimate received, pending technician review
- **Scheduled** - Appointment confirmed with date/time
- **Ready** - Revv Report received, job ready for calibration
- **In Progress** - Technician actively working on vehicle
- **Completed** - Invoice received, all documents present, job closed
- **Cancelled** - Job cancelled by shop or tech

Note: "Not Ready", "Needs Attention", and "Blocked" statuses are DEPRECATED.

1. **Estimate** - Sent by shop to RadarSolutions email
2. **Revv Report** - Generated by technician after manual scrub in RevvADAS
3. **Post Scan** - Completed by technician after calibration
