# Third-Party Platforms Folder

## Purpose
Information on third-party repair information platforms and ADAS calibration equipment providers.

## Platform Categories

### Repair Information Platforms

| Platform | Best For | Monthly Cost |
|----------|----------|--------------|
| ALLDATA | Industry standard OEM data | $189-229 |
| Mitchell1 ProDemand | Real-world fixes | ~$199 |
| Identifix Direct-Hit | Diagnostic direction | $209-229 |
| ShopKey Pro | Snap-on users | $145-199 |
| RepairLogic | OEM-direct collision procedures | Contact |
| I-CAR RTS | ADAS calibration requirements | Membership |
| OEM1Stop | FREE portal hub | FREE |

## ADAS Quality Comparison

**Excellent ADAS Coverage:**
- ALLDATA (OEM-licensed)
- Mitchell1 ProDemand (SureTrack)
- RepairLogic (OEM-direct)
- I-CAR RTS (first calibration database)

**Good ADAS Coverage:**
- Identifix (developing)
- Bosch ESI[tronic] (European)
- HaynesPro (European)

**Limited ADAS:**
- Autodata (basic)
- Chilton/Haynes (minimal)

## Folder Contents

Each platform folder should contain:
- Platform overview and pricing
- Coverage by brand
- ADAS coverage assessment
- Integration capabilities
- Known gaps vs OEM data
- Contact information

## Recommendations

### For General Repair Shops
Primary: ALLDATA or Mitchell1
Reference: I-CAR RTS for calibration requirements

### For Collision Shops
Primary: CCC ONE (estimating) + RepairLogic (procedures)
Reference: I-CAR RTS

### For ADAS Calibration
Equipment: Autel (mobile) or Hunter (fixed)
Data: OEM subscriptions + I-CAR RTS

### For European Specialists
Primary: Bosch ESI[tronic] or HaynesPro
Equipment: Bosch DAS 3000 or Hella Gutmann
